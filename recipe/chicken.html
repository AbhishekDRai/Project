Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides, about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Transfer chicken breasts to a baking dish and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through.
  6. Remove chicken from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.
  7. Top chicken with garlic, parsley, and basil before serving.
